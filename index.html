Serving Kelowna & the Okanagan, BC

Community Support Services for Adults with Developmental Disabilities in Kelowna & the Okanagan

Evercare Support Services provides compassionate, person-centered community support designed to promote independence, dignity, safety, and inclusion. We help adults with developmental disabilities live fuller lives at home and in the community across Kelowna, West Kelowna, Lake Country, Penticton, and the wider Okanagan.

What We Offer

Our Community Support Services

Evercare Support Services delivers flexible, individualized support in client homes and community settings across the Okanagan. Every service is built around the person we support — their goals, strengths, routines, and needs. Explore each service below.

Community Inclusion Support

Support to participate in community activities, build confidence, strengthen social connections, and develop practical life skills in Kelowna and the Okanagan.

Learn more →

Daily Living Support

Assistance with daily routines and practical tasks such as meal preparation, organization, appointments, and independent living skills.

Learn more →

Supported Living / Outreach

Individualized, goal-directed support to help people maintain or increase independence while living in community-based settings.

Learn more →

Companion Support

Respectful one-to-one support that encourages engagement, emotional connection, confidence, and routine stability.

Learn more →

Respite Support

Short-term, dependable relief for families and caregivers while maintaining quality and continuity of care for the individual.

Learn more →

Family-Centered Support

Collaborative support that works with families and support networks to create stability, trust, and continuity in service delivery.

Personalized Support Services

Flexible support tailored to the goals, needs, strengths, and routines of each individual — fully responsive to changing needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions From Families

What areas does Evercare Support Services serve?

We deliver community-based support across Kelowna, West Kelowna, Lake Country, Penticton, and the wider Okanagan region of British Columbia, in client homes and community settings.

Who does Evercare Support Services help?

We provide person-centered support for adults with developmental disabilities, helping them build independence, participate in their community, and live with dignity and safety.

What services does Evercare provide?

Our services include community inclusion support, daily living support, supported living and outreach, companion support, respite support, family-centered support, and personalized support services.

How do I request support services?

Complete the inquiry form on this page, email info@evercaresupport.ca, or message us on WhatsApp. We will follow up to arrange a consultation and discuss your needs.
