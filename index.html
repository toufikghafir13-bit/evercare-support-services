About Evercare

Dependable Support Built Around the Individual

Evercare Support Services is a British Columbia-based provider focused on respectful, person-centred support in homes and community settings. Our work is guided by dignity, safety, choice, inclusion and meaningful progress.

We collaborate with individuals, families and support networks to build practical plans that reflect real goals, routines, preferences and changing needs.
