About Evercare

Compassionate Support You Can Rely On

Evercare Support Services is a British Columbia-based community support company focused on delivering dependable, respectful, and person-centered care in homes and community settings across the Okanagan.

We focus on helping individuals live with dignity, independence, safety, and meaningful inclusion. Our services are designed to support daily living, community participation, respite, supported living, and individualized care planning.

Our Mission: To provide high-quality, compassionate, and person-centered support that improves quality of life, promotes independence, and strengthens community inclusion.

Our Vision: To become a trusted support provider in British Columbia known for dependable care, strong values, and meaningful impact in the lives of individuals and families.

What We Offer

Our Support Services

Flexible, individualized services delivered in client homes and community settings across the Okanagan.

Community Inclusion Support

Support for individuals to participate in community activities, develop confidence, strengthen social connection, and build practical life skills.

  • Improves social participation
  • Builds confidence and routine
  • Supports meaningful community presence

Daily Living Support

Assistance with routines and practical day-to-day tasks such as meal preparation, organization, appointments, and independent living skills.

  • Encourages independence
  • Improves consistency in routines
  • Supports practical skill development

Supported Living / Outreach

Individualized, goal-directed support designed to help people maintain or increase independence while living in community-based settings.

  • Promotes autonomy
  • Supports community navigation
  • Builds independent living capacity

Companion Support

Respectful one-to-one support that encourages engagement, emotional connection, confidence, and routine stability.

  • Reduces isolation
  • Improves engagement
  • Creates continuity and trust

Respite Support

Short-term support that gives families and caregivers dependable relief while maintaining quality and continuity of care.

  • Reduces caregiver stress
  • Provides reliable temporary coverage
  • Maintains consistency for the individual

Family-Centered Support

Collaborative support that works with families and support networks to create stability, trust, and continuity in service delivery.

  • Strengthens communication
  • Aligns support with family goals
  • Improves care consistency
