Our Mission

Empowering Lives with Compassion and Professional Care

At Evercare Support Services, we believe that every individual deserves a life filled with dignity, opportunity, and meaningful connection. Based in Kelowna, our team is dedicated to providing person-centered support tailored to the unique goals and aspirations of adults with developmental disabilities.

By fostering a community of belonging and respect, we help our clients navigate their daily lives with confidence, ensuring they have the tools and support needed to thrive in their local environment.
