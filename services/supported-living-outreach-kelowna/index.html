Supported Living & Outreach Support in Kelowna & the Okanagan

Evercare Support Services provides individualized supported living and outreach support that helps adults with developmental disabilities maintain or increase their independence while living in community-based settings across Kelowna and the Okanagan.

What Is Supported Living & Outreach Support?

Supported living and outreach support is goal-directed support delivered to people living in their own community-based settings. Rather than a fixed program, it flexes around each person’s life — offering the right level of support to build independence, navigate the community, and reach personal goals.

Outreach support often means meeting people where they are: at home, in the neighbourhood, or out in the community. The focus is on capability and autonomy, helping individuals do more for themselves over time with dependable support along the way.

What This Support Can Include

  • Goal-setting and building independent living capacity
  • Support navigating community services and resources
  • Encouraging autonomy and decision-making
  • Connecting to community, recreation, and routines
  • Flexible support that adapts as goals evolve

Independence With Dependable Backup

Our model promotes autonomy while ensuring individuals are never left without support. We work closely with each person and their network to keep support relevant, respectful, and centred on their goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between supported living and outreach?

Both are flexible, community-based supports. Supported living focuses on day-to-day independence in a person’s own setting, while outreach often involves meeting people in the community to work toward specific goals. We tailor the mix to each individual.

Who is this support for?

It supports adults with developmental disabilities who live in community-based settings and want to build or maintain independence with reliable support.

Where is this offered?

Across Kelowna, West Kelowna, Lake Country, Penticton, and the wider Okanagan, BC.

Complete the inquiry form on this page, email info@evercaresupport.ca, or message us on WhatsApp to arrange a consultation.
