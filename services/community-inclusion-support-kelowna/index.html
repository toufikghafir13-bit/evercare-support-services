Community Inclusion Support in Kelowna & the Okanagan

Evercare Support Services helps adults with developmental disabilities take part in community life with confidence. Our community inclusion support builds social connection, practical skills, and a meaningful, valued presence in Kelowna and across the Okanagan.

What Is Community Inclusion Support?

Community inclusion support helps individuals participate in the activities, places, and relationships that matter to them. Rather than support that keeps people apart, our approach is about connection — joining community programs, building friendships, learning to navigate local spaces, and developing the confidence to belong.

Every person is different, so our support is shaped around individual goals, interests, and routines. Whether the focus is volunteering, recreation, classes, social groups, or simply getting comfortable in busy public spaces, we support participation in a way that respects each person's pace and preferences.

How We Support Community Participation

  • Identifying interests, strengths, and meaningful community goals
  • Accompanying individuals to programs, activities, and outings
  • Building practical skills like using transit, ordering at a cafe, or joining a group
  • Encouraging social connection and natural friendships
  • Growing confidence step by step in real community settings

Person-Centered & Community-Based

Support is delivered where life happens — in the community, not just indoors. We focus on dignity, choice, and independence, working alongside families and support networks so progress is consistent and meaningful over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is community inclusion support for?

It supports adults with developmental disabilities who want to participate more fully in community activities, build social connections, and develop practical life skills.

Where does the support take place?

Support is community-based and delivered in the places that matter to each person across Kelowna, West Kelowna, Lake Country, Penticton, and the wider Okanagan.

How do you decide which activities to focus on?

We start with each individual’s interests, strengths, and goals, then build a flexible, person-centered plan that can adapt as confidence and preferences grow.

How do I request community inclusion support?

Complete the inquiry form on this page, email info@evercaresupport.ca, or message us on WhatsApp, and we will arrange a consultation.
